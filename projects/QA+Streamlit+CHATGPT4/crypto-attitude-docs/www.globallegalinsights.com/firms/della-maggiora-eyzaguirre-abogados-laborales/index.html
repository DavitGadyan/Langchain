Firms Della Maggiora Eyzaguirre Abogados Laborales

Della Maggiora Eyzaguirre Abogados Laborales

Della Maggiora Eyzaguirre Abogados is a Chilean law firm based in Santiago de Chile specialising in advising national and foreign companies on labour, immigration and social security matters. 

The firm’s core expertise lies in labour relations, litigation, collective bargaining processes, individual and mass terminations, human resources restructuring processes, executive compensation systems, remuneration and benefits, internal policies, audits, employee taxation, pensions, and health and safety.

Della Maggiora Eyzaguirre Abogados primarily serves clients operating in diverse industries, including mining, energy, construction, real estate, agriculture, engineering, education, financial services, computing, transportation and manufacturing.

Recognised for its capabilities in labour and employment law, the firm has received numerous awards and accolades, including the “Labour and Employment Law Firm of the Year” award by Best Lawyers in 2024. Additionally, it has been acknowledged by Leaders League and featured by Latin Lawyer 250 2024 as a recommended firm.

Among the firms notable clients are leading entities such as Bechtel Chile Ltda., Sandvik Chile S.A., Sandvik Mining & Construction Chile S.A., Generadora Metropolitana SpA, Obrascn Huarte Lain (OHLA), Molymetnos S.A., Engie Energa S.A. and Subsidiaries, EDF Andes SpA, EuroAmrica S.A., Hidronor Chile S.A., and General Motors Financial Chile Ltda.
